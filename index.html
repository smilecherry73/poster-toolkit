🔍 Reflective Q&A Prompt Bank

Use during post-presentation Q&A or as peer review card prompts.

🖼️ Visual & Structure

  • If you could keep only one figure, which would it be and why?
  • When viewed as a thumbnail, what information is recognized first?
  • Where does the eye land in the first 5 seconds? Does it match your intention?

📖 Scientific Narrative

  • Are you telling a detective story or submitting a lab report?
  • If an editor asked you to highlight a secondary finding, how would you reorganize?
  • Is your conclusion based on data or on interpretation? Is the boundary clear?

🗣️ Oral Translation

  • Whose understanding did your preview change? Was that your intended audience?
  • How would you explain the value of this study to a patient's family in one sentence?
  • Did your pace change when explaining complex figures? Intentional or nerves?

🤝 Engagement & Adaptability

  • Did any question make you feel challenged? How did you respond internally?
  • If the audience asked about conclusion B instead of A, what does that reveal?
  • Was there a moment you wanted to interrupt? What does that impulse tell you?

🎭 Delivery Reflection

  • Where did your gaze predominantly rest? How did the audience react?
  • Did your gestures reinforce the message or distract from it?
  • Which rhetorical device (analogy, repetition, etc.) was most effective?

🧭 Instructor Feedback Entry Points & Operations

📱 Concept Correction

Action Photograph the poster with a phone and shrink to screen size to test core visibility.

🏷️ Title Intervention

Contrast Descriptive title vs. conclusion-driven title; rewrite on the spot.

👁️ Visual Clinic

Eye Exam Project the poster; have the back row identify the smallest font size.

🎙️ Oral Polishing

Drill Point to the figure with the laser pointer while simultaneously explaining its meaning.

🛡️ Defensive Responding

Phrasing "If I understand correctly, you are asking..." — model academic dialogue.

🎭 Delivery Intervention

Eye Contact Assign three audience members in different zones.
